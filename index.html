Abstract:

The aim of this project is to allow for two Android devices to communicate with each other using only their screens and cameras. Assuming their screens and cameras are on the same side, by getting two phones close to each other (e.g., tens of cm), you can have them exchange information. The big picture is simple, the user on one device inputs a message in a text field and then QR codes are generated on a sentence by sentence basis by the sender and the receiver scans these using its front camera, extracts each of the sentences and finally displays the message. The main issue to tackle in this design is the synchronization between the sender and the designer. The receiver needs to know where the message starts and ends correctly in order to successfully decode the codes.
